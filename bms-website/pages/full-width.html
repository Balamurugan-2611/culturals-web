Coordinators' Desk

Sri Sairam Institute of Technology is a center of excellence dedicated to fostering innovation and talent among students. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, an inspiring learning environment, and a strong focus on holistic education, the institution continues to excel in academics, cultural activities, and societal contributions.

Under the visionary leadership of Dr Sai Prakash LeoMuthu, our CEO, the institution has reached remarkable milestones.

His dedication to nurturing creativity and promoting excellence inspires both staff and students to consistently achieve greater heights. His efforts have transformed the campus into a thriving hub for innovation, inclusivity, and empowerment.

The success of our cultural fest lies in the hands of an extraordinary team working tirelessly behind the scenes. This team, composed of passionate staff and enthusiastic student coordinators, ensures every aspect of the event runs seamlessly.
